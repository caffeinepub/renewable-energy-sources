# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Make the renewable energy educational pages easier to read (lower high school level) and add a Biofuels process diagram consistent with the other pages’ diagrams.

**Planned changes:**
- Simplify all explanatory paragraph text on the Hydropower & Tidal page while keeping all headings and section structure unchanged and leaving the existing diagram as-is.
- Simplify all explanatory paragraph text on the Wind Energy page while keeping all headings and section structure unchanged and leaving the existing diagram as-is.
- Simplify all explanatory paragraph text on the Geothermal Energy page while keeping all headings and section structure unchanged and leaving the existing diagram as-is.
- Simplify all explanatory paragraph text on the Biomass Energy page while keeping all headings and section structure unchanged and leaving the existing diagram as-is.
- Simplify all explanatory paragraph text on the Biofuels page while keeping all headings and section structure unchanged and covering the same topics as before.
- Add a new Biofuels process diagram component (matching the styling/accessibility patterns of existing SVG diagram components) and render it at the bottom of the Biofuels page content.
- Simplify user-facing paragraph text and per-source descriptions on the Sources page while keeping all source titles and URLs exactly the same and leaving layout/link behavior unchanged.

**User-visible outcome:** The Hydropower & Tidal, Wind, Geothermal, Biomass, Biofuels, and Sources pages read more clearly at a lower high school level, and the Biofuels page includes a new process diagram at the bottom similar to the other energy pages.
